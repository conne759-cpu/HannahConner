Hello, I'm Hannah!

I'm a passionate undergraduate student at UMD, pursuing my degree in Psychology with a focus on Environmental Psychology. My journey combines my love for understanding human behavior with my deep connection to nature and environmental conservation.

I'm particularly interested in studying how our environment impacts mental health and well-being, and I aspire to contribute to creating more nature-integrated therapeutic approaches. Through my studies and research, I aim to bridge the gap between psychological well-being and environmental consciousness.
